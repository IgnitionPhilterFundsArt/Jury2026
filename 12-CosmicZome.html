12 - Cosmic Zome

Description for the Jury

The Cosmic Zome is a 13 foot tall, 20 foot diameter, projection-mapped zome that debuted at Flipside 2024. Unlike traditional geodesic domes which are built out of triangles, a zome is a similar structure built from kite shapes instead of triangles. This small twist creates a beautiful shape suggesting sacred geometry, with the top of the zome forming the petals of a flower. The wooden frame is covered with 84 panels, and 4 projectors inside the zome create a 360 degree continuous video wall for visitors to enjoy. Hundreds (thousands?) or Flipizens have stopped by to lay down and look up at the roof of the structure to relax and get lost in the immersive video experience. The 2026 update to the Cosmic Zome will add a bass-reactive floor to the project. When people lay down to enjoy the video show and music, they will also FEEL the music through 13 bass shakers installed under the floor. Music will come from external sources (e.g. DJ stage music) as well as custom playlists (e.g. ambient bass music) designed to provide attendees with an even more amazing experience.
