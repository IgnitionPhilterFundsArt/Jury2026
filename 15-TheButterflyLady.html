15 - The Butterfly Lady

Description for the Jury

This piece is a continuation on my previous work in puppetry and interactive art. This sculpture is a crank style puppet with poseable limbs and moveable wings. The limbs will be built out of plywood in the style of a paper puppet. The wings will have the crank mechanism allowing the viewer to play with the sculpture and bring it to life. I love creating supernatural creatures, it is very nostalgic because I grew up creating my own creatures and making paper puppets . I"m hoping to bring my previous puppet and the two pieces can interact with each other adding to the whimsical experience!
