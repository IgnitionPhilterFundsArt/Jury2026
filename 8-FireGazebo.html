8 - Fire Gazebo

Description for the Jury

The fire gazebo is a place of power, gathering, and reflection. Participants approach a glowing structure greeted by solar lanterns hanging around and reflecting patterns on the ground. They enter through one of two entrances to find a relaxation area of rugs and cushions with backboards to lean against around the sides of the structure. In the center is a small altar and hanging above a chandelier. The roof of the gazebo glows with a continuous flame effect running along each bar at the top. Participants look around and see prayer/thought/poetry/manifestation cards on each of the 8 posts of the gazebo accompanied by an item/d"cor. Four of the 8 gazebo posts also have a button that when pushed triggers one of four high pressure propane effect to go off overhead.

Safety Plan

Safety plan includes leak testing all components, multiple easily accessible shut off valves in each system, having a trained sober operator on site at all times the project is running, fire extinguisher, appropriate distance of fuel station from ignition, and meeting any other safety standards set forth by the event.
