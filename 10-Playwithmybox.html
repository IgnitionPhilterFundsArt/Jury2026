10 - Play with my box

Description for the Jury

This will be a 4-sided interactive LED piece. It will be off the ground 3 or 4 feet for optimum viewing and the over height will be 6 or 7 feet tall (each illuminated panel will be 3 feet x 3 feet on each of the 4 faces). Each side is capable of displaying different colors or patterns at the same time. There are 100 individually addressable LED nodes used on each side (total of 400). All of the LED nodes use ping pong balls as diffusers and each ball is held by a 3d printed ring and vinyl tubing to space the balls over the face of the panel. The 3d printer filament that I will be using has UV properties that make the ball supports glow when certain color light is selected. I will incorporate push buttons so participants can toggle through different settings and patterns. The lighting is also capable of reacting to sound if there is a music source nearby. Additionally I will be able to integrate this piece with a display I created a couple years ago for one unique and fun time! The included photos show one completed panel in daylight mode, one with panel lit, and a closeup of the ping pong ball diffuser and 3d printed support ring.
